ABOUT US

Located in Danvers Massachusetts, Thermo Wave is a team built on more than a century of combined experience in the field of High Power Microwave and RF Science and Engineering. Our Service, Engineering and Operations staff have direct knowledge and hands-on experience with all Ferrite, Amana and Raytheon Systems

Thermo Wave’s Industrial Microwave Systems Division provides Engineers, Designers, Manufacturing Specialists, and Programmers for delivery of Equipment, Spare Parts and Service with turnkey solutions into a broad range of multi-mode and single-mode microwave applications.
