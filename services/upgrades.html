UPGRADES

Upgrades to Microwave Systems and Components

Upgrades to a Microwave System will mean different things to different users. There are many Microwave Systems still operating with their original or many of their original parts. A well maintained and operated Microwave system should give many years of efficient operation, but when they tire, or a more efficient method of operation is found, then it may be time to upgrade.

Thermo Wave Technologies is at the cutting edge of Industrial Microwave design and application. We have developed a series of Upgrades to allow our customers to take advantage of some the latest advances in Microwave Technology within their current Microwave configurations.

Synthesized Polar System (SPS)

Thermo Wave has also developed the Polar Feed System, Called the SPS (Synthesized Polar System). Currently available in 2 variants (SPS Folded Feed and SPS Advanced Feed).

We take a systemic approach to the Microwave environment when determining placement of the feeds. This approach in conjunction with our Advanced Polar Technology has resulted in the most uniform circular microwave output in the industry today. These Feeds are already in use on cooking systems in the bacon industry and many Tempering and Boost Systems - and the results are outstanding. The SPS Folded Feed can be a direct replacement for rotating antenna feeds also.

STEP START

Reduces voltage “Inrush” at start up. Many other advantages are detailed below including saving $$$$ due to demand charges.

The Step Start Upgrade is a notable development. As the name suggests, the Step Start allows the power supply’s high voltage transformer to “power up” in stages. This is a device designed to provide a reduced voltage at start up, reducing inrush.

Another benefit is that secondary voltage is also reduced, reducing the stress on diodes and Magnetron Tube. This in hand can improve the service life of tubes and diodes, which results in less down time and reduced maintenance costs.
