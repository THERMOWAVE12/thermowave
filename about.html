About Us

Our Mission

hermo Wave is dedicated to delivering absolutely optimum results to our customers. That means providing all Systems and Components based on the latest innovative and efficient Technologies, Manufacturing Quality and customer requirements responsiveness.

Our Business

Thermo Wave’s Industrial Microwave Systems Division provides Engineers, Designers, Manufacturing Specialists, and Programmers for delivery of Equipment, Spare Parts and Service with turnkey solutions into a broad range of multi-mode and single-mode microwave applications. Our vertical manufacturing capability includes a full fabrication house, Thermo craft engineering, which handles all of our sheet metal fabrication and machining .

Principle Products & Services

Thermo Wave provides Modular Systems for Drying, Tempering, Cooking and Heating applications using the benefits offered by microwave energy. We can supply parts, service and technical support for any microwave system in use in the world today.

Markets

Thermo Wave markets include but are not limited to the Food, Lumber, Engineering Materials, Waste Stream Management, Textile, Environmental and Energy Applications.

Our Team

Thermo Wave is a team built on more than a century of combined experience in the field of High Power Microwave Systems, Service and Engineering.

Our Facilities

Thermo Wave’s main office is located in Danvers MA. This includes our Sales and Engineering staff as well Inventory and Sub-Assembly Manufacturing. Thermo Wave’s System Manufacturing and Test is performed in our Peabody MA facility.
