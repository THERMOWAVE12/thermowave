Industrial Food Microwave Systems

Thermo Wave Technologies continues to be the leader in innovation in the Industrial Microwave Industry. Without the shackles of tired and obsolete thinking, we have developed a series of Upgrades to allow our customers to take advantage of some the latest advances in Microwave Technology within their current Microwave configurations. Results have been outstanding– see our industry advancements below.

CT-XXX, Food Tempering

The CT Series of Industrial Food Tempering equipment can provide precision heating for a wide array of frozen bulk food products. Systems configured specifically for beef block, vegetable, fruit juice and fish block tempering are available. The use of microwave tempering promotes real time tempering to a very accurate final temperature allowing deep frozen products to be immediately used on the production floor.

BT-75, Food Tempering

Batch tempering allows for an economical entry point for Industrial Bulk Food Tempering in near real time. With the ability to handle up to 8,000 lbs. per hour of frozen product, the BT Microwave system can be used to batch temper widely different products, batch to batch with absolutely no down time.
